How it started

Our Mission

At Ameri-Steam, we are committed to shaping the future by empowering the young minds of today. We believe that through our educational enrichment programs, personalized mentoring, and leadership training, we can instill the necessary skills and confidence in our students, helping them make a significant positive impact on their communities and the world at large.

Our mission also encompasses the larger goal of developing stable, prosperous communities. We understand that the long-term prosperity of a community is heavily reliant on the skills, aptitude, and leadership qualities of its leaders. We take immense pride in being a catalyst for change and fostering an environment where young minds are free to explore, innovate, and lead with confidence. At Ameri-Steam, the young minds of today will become the leaders of tomorrow.

Why Ameri-STEAM

Objectives

1. Find and partner with local and international public and private organizations to provide opportunities and resources that expand girls' access to internships and training, field trips, international travel and mentorship programs

2. Empower young girls with the skills and qualifications to apply for and be accepted into the best universities in the world and return to their communities to lead and leave a legacy.

3. Introduce and match young girls with world leaders as mentors through group and individual mentoring programs. To equip young girls with the skills and training to become great global citizens, responsible and influential leaders and agents of positive change.

Why Ameri-STEAM

Objectives

1. Find and partner with local and international public and private organizations to provide opportunities and resources that expand girls' access to internships and training, field trips, international travel and mentorship programs

2. Empower young girls with the skills and qualifications to apply for and be accepted into the best universities in the world and return to their communities to lead and leave a legacy.

3. Introduce and match young girls with world leaders as mentors through group and individual mentoring programs. To equip young girls with the skills and training to become great global citizens, responsible and influential leaders and agents of positive change.
